See me play music!

The Skylark, Isabella Li

This is an excerpt of recording for the piano Level 9 test at the Central Conservatory of Music. I trained really hard for this and it taught me how perseverance and practice could make dreams come true.

Orchestra excerpt, Variations of Emotion, 3rd Movement, Tsinghua University Primary School Golden Sail Orchestra

Here is a video of the award-winning music my orchestra members and I played in 2019. I played as the first chair of the dulcimer section. We passed numerous levels of competition and won the Golden Award in China in the National Youth Art Festival. We rehearsed for hours every day and it was the dream of all the young orchastra musicians.

Lin Chong, Solo, Isabella Li

This is one of my favorite dulcimer songs telling a story about a chivalrous hero. He is a fictional character from a classical Chinese novel "Water Margins", and the music can adequately express even his tiniest change in emotions, from repent to anger, from mild to roused.

    Demands
    Consumer preferences: though Studio Ghibli only makes movies once every 4-5 years, the decrease in supply increases their scarcity.
     
    Restricting supply
    Unlike firms that respond to rising demand by increasing output (positive supply curve shift), Ghibli restricts supply to preserve artistic quality.
     
    AI Art
    AI Ghibli art decreases its scarcity and lower demand, despite sentiments for disrespect. It has still not been fixed.
